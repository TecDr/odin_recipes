How to Prepare Bitter leaf soup
Ingredients
  • cocoyam seeds
  • Meats
  • Bitter leaf Vegetables
  • Maggi
  • Salt
  • Water
  • Stock Fish
Steps
  1. Pour water inside a pot
  2. Put the pot on fire and allow to warm
  3. Put palm oil inside the pot and allow it to boil
  4. Then put the Cocoyam seeds inside the pot and allow it for some time
  5. Then add other ingredients
